#### 1. The Tech Interactive
- **What**: Hands-on technology and science museum
- **Time Needed**: 3-4 hours
- **Cost**: $35 adults, $28 children; IMAX additional $10
- **Why Visit**: World-class interactive exhibits on Silicon Valley innovation
- **Best Time**: Mornings on weekends (less crowded)
- **Address**: 201 S Market St, Downtown

#### 2. San Jose Museum of Art
- **What**: Contemporary and modern art museum
- **Time Needed**: 1.5-2 hours
- **Cost**: $10 adults (free first Fridays)
- **Why Visit**: Excellent rotating exhibitions, focus on West Coast artists
- **Best Time**: Sunday mornings
- **Address**: 110 S Market St, Downtown

#### 3. Rosicrucian Egyptian Museum
- **What**: Largest collection of Egyptian artifacts in western North America
- **Time Needed**: 2 hours
- **Cost**: $12 adults
- **Why Visit**: Unique collection, beautiful gardens, planetarium
- **Best Time**: Afternoon
- **Address**: 1660 Park Ave

#### 4. Japanese Friendship Garden (Kelley Park)
- **What**: Beautiful 6-acre traditional Japanese garden
- **Time Needed**: 1 hour
- **Cost**: $10 adults
- **Why Visit**: Peaceful koi ponds, tea house, authentic design
- **Best Time**: Mid-morning for best light
- **Address**: 1300 Senter Rd

#### 5. Santana Row
- **What**: Upscale outdoor shopping and dining district
- **Time Needed**: 2-3 hours
- **Cost**: Free to browse
- **Why Visit**: Beautiful European-style architecture, excellent restaurants
- **Best Time**: Evening for ambiance and dining

#### 6. San Pedro Square Market

- **Time Needed**: 1-2 hours
